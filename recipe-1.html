Perfection Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 envelopes unflavored gelatine
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 can (12 oz) apple juice
  • 1/2 cup lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar
  • 1 cup shredded carrot
  • 1 cup sliced celery
  • 1 cup finely shredded cabbage
  • 1/2 cup chopped green pepper
  • 1 can (4 oz) chopped pimiento

Preparation

  1. In small saucepan, combine gelatine, sugar, and salt; mix well.

  2. Add 1 cup water. Heat over low heat, stirring constantly, until sugar and gelatine are dissolved.

  3. Remove from heat.

  4. Stir in apple juice, lemon juice, vinegar, and 1/4 cup cold water.

  5. Pour into medium bowl.

  6. Refrigerate 1 hour, or until mixture is consistency of unbeaten egg white.

  7. Add carrot, celery, cabbage, green pepper, and pimiento; stir until well combined.

  8. Turn into decorative, 1 1/2-quart mold.

  9. Refrigerate 4 hours,or until firm.

    10. To unmold:

  10. Run small spatula around edge of mold; invert onto serving plate.

  11. Place hot dishcloth over mold; shake gently to release. Repeat, if necessary.

  12. Lift off mold. refrigerate until ready to serve.

  13. Make 8 servings
